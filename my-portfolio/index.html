About Me

Melodie is a human being who doesn't know how to sufficiently describe herself or write a short personal biography. She often finds herself thinking way too much about things that, in reality, will never affect her.

Her major interests include creative writing, psychology, philosophy, singing-songwriting, doodling, design, photography, and of course, web development.

She plans to graduate from UCI & the UCI Coding Bootcamp, land a job, move to San Diego with her two furry friends, and work remotely while playing guitar and traveling with the sea breeze.
